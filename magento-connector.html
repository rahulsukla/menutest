Simple by design. Fast in performance. Beautiful to work with.

Inception OpenERP Tryton

When we thought of building a Magento ERP integration, we pictured you with a solution that was simple, stable, fast and beautiful. With that image in mind, we started designing the integration and created a solution, that gives you total control of your webstore and frees you from worrying about the big as well as the little details that make your process unique. So now, you can relax and leave the complex stuff to the integration.

Since we contribute to the Open Source sphere, we focussed on the two foremost ERP’s in that region, OpenERP and Tryton. OpenERP has a vast array of modules, which relate to various aspects of a web store. From inventory management to accounting details, OpenERP has it all.

Tryton is known as one of the most stable ERP in the world and we did not want to miss out on integrating Magento with Tryton. In addition to the stability, Tryton Magento integration provides many additional features so that you can apply your expertise in other areas of managing a webstore.

Error free fulfilment of orders. No more expensive return shipments.

Accurate Efficient Integrated Shipping

Managing a webstore can be a stressful job and we know that. Finalising order details like multiple products, discount and shipping costs can turn out to to be a problematic job. The ERP system imports every relevant detail of the order with 100% accuracy. With an ERP integration, you will never go wrong in fulfilling an order from the moment the customer places an order to the shipping of the product.

How annoyed would your customer be if you shipped a PlayStation title instead of the ordered Xbox title? You will never have to find out as the ERP system handles the process efficiently with zero errors. With the absence of manual errors, the integration guarantees an organised approach towards the fulfillment of the order of your customer.

Shipping forms a major part of your order fulfillment process. You can integrate shipping providers, like Fedex, UPS and USPS with the ERP system and print accurate labels, thus delivering the right product without any hiccups.

Purchase and deliver with ease. Build lasting relationships with your suppliers.

Streamline Purchases Drop Shipment Enhanced Supplier Association

Now, there is no need to create purchase orders manually, you can generate them automatically using the ERP. The system sends you an alert when the stock is depleted and you can take the necessary action. The details of the vendor are stored in the database, which stops duplication of data. It gives you a whole new level of peace of mind.

Drop shipment helps you to deliver products even if you don’t have them in your stock. The ERP system allows you to ship the product directly from the supplier/manufacturer to the customer without coming to your stock. You can provide the customers a wide variety of products and fulfill them using the ERP via drop shipments.

The Magento ERP integration also allows users throughout your enterprise to understand your suppliers better. Your employees can access the information about the services and processes of your suppliers helping them to increase efficiency across different levels. You don’t have to spend heavily on the training of your employees, the ERP system does the job.

Accounting was never this easy. Generate reports to aid your decisions.

Simplified Accounting Generate Reports

Accounting will never be intimidating again. The integration makes the complex world of numbers simple and presents it in a easy-to-do format. There will be no delay in the payments and you can generate invoices based on purchase and sales orders. Suddenly, you spend less time on accounting and more time on your business.

Reports generated through the Magento ERP integration help you to take informed purchasing decisions based on complete order history analysis. You have accurate information at your fingertips to monitor outbound delivery through the integration.

Improved inventory management. Better additional sales channels support.

Intelligent Additional Sales Channels Instantly live inventory

The ERP system when integrated with Magento allows you to manage the catalogue of your webstore effectively. You don’t have to change the status of the product based on the stock manually. The ERP system updates the product status as available and unavailable based on the stock.

Management of products on additional sales channels like, Amazon, eBay and Play, becomes easy with the help of Magento ERP integration. When your stock is depleted, the ERP system will automatically inform the multiple channels about your stock levels. So your work is reduced.

You will feel that management of inventory cannot get easier after the Magento ERP integration. With instant live inventory management your stock levels are accurate across additional channels and your own webstore.
